Why Just Ask Major

A dad, a son, and a mission that changed everything.

Major is the reason everything changed. Raising a child with special needs opens your eyes to a world where technology can either create barriers or break them down — and it showed Marc which side he wanted to be on.

With over 15 years in enterprise IT and cloud infrastructure, Marc brings the technical depth of a Senior Cloud Systems Administrator to the conversations that families and nonprofits need most. Not help desk jargon. Not confusing tech-speak. Real guidance from someone who gets it — because he lives it.

Through H.E.R.O.E.S. DFW and speaking engagements across the Dallas area, Marc has made it his mission to ensure that no family feels alone when technology gets in the way of connection, care, and community.
